Letter Substitution
Hover for rules 1. Enter a word in the Text box at the top (punctuated, numbered words, and sentences are will not continue the game.)
(Some words will not be in this free dictionary)
2. Press Enter or select the 'Submit' button, if it's a valid word it will be separated into component letters.
3. Select a letter you wish to substitute in that word.
4. Select a valid word in the grid below and that word will be the new word!
5. Repeat!
